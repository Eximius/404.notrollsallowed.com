--[ SOUND ]

We're going to kick off the sound program with the screening of the "Dusk chorus
(2017)" movie on Thursday evening as a gentle reminder of the importance of
stopping and simply listening. It is only timely, as the decay of Earth is only
picking up its pace, and it seems that the only way to recall extinct species in
the future will be by listening to these crackling field recordings from the old
days ...

Once the initial set of talks completes on Friday, we'll gradually vanish into
the upcoming weekend mist with breezy tropical tunes and slow hypnotic rhythms.

For those still in need of taking the edge off, the sound therapy on Saturday
will pick up its pace, and we're going to temporarily leave the madness behind
following the fast and wicked thuds into the night.

Last but not least, we're going to keep the 'Dance till the police come' record
by Peverelist handy.
